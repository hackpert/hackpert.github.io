SumUp is a user-friendly application developed in Python that can generate a short summary of any article or long piece of text. It utilises a variation of the PageRank[1] algorithm along with vectorizable linguistic metrics like word frequency and Term Frequency - Inverse Document Frequency [2] to determine how essential a sentence is to the text. It then generates a responsive webpage containing the summary of the article along with relevant images and videos. The user is provided with options to enter a URL or paste the entire text, and is then prompted for an estimated summary length.
The front-end was built using the Tkinter[3] module and in HTML/CSS.

[1] PageRank. Larry Page et al. 1997 [2] Tf-idf [3] Tkinter
