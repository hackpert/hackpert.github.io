Sudoku puzzles have become a fascination in the past few years. PyDoku is a computer vision based program that uses OpenCV to recognise and interpret sudoku puzzles in printed format, digitises them, and can also solve them. It detects the grid in an image, flattens it out, and recognises and fills the numbers using Optical Character Recognition. The puzzle is then solved using a simple search algorithm.
