Teenagers today spend a lot of time thinking about the right hashtags to post with their pictures on social networks that are relevant and maximize popularity. To combat this problem, we came up with a machine learning model based on the VGGnet, comprising a Convolutional Neural Network and an LSTM trained on data obtained by scraping 1,000,000 popular pictures from Instagram. The software was able to generate accurate and relevant hashtags for nearly every image input, and was thus released for public usage, gaining popularity quickly.
The back-end was coded in MATLAB and deployed as an API, and the front-end was made using HTML and PHP. It was integrated with Instagram and Facebook APIs to post directly on these social networks.
