IndoWrite

Software that recognizes handwritten Devanagri script and converts it to editable UTF-8 text. 2016

Onboard positioning in drone swarms

A hardware attachment for flying machines to perform relative positioning in swarms using radio transcievers. 2016

VQA

Research on a program that can answer questions on any image using a neural network to make images accessible to the visually impaired. 2015-present

Created as a part of internship in the Advanced Research Group at Wolfram Research Inc.

Sum_Up

Automatic text summarizer in Python based on Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency metric and PageRank. 2015

Created as a final project for CBSE Computer Science Grade XII

EarlyDetect

App that uses convolutional neural networks to diagnose 7 types of cancer in early stages using photographs. 2014-15

Project selected for Google Science Fair 2015 Finals, Intel ISEF 2015 Semi-finals
Published in Journal of Machine Learning Research

Research on a novel bioplastic

Invented a new type of bioplastic based on cross-linked starch polymers extracted from organic waste. 2013

LSTM Melody Generator

A program based on LSTM that generates new .midi melodies, trained on classical music. 2015
