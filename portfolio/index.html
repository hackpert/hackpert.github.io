Here are a few things I've worked on

EarlyDetect

App that uses convolutional neural networks to diagnose 7 types of cancer in early stages using photographs. 2014-15

Project selected for Google Science Fair 2015 Finals, Intel ISEF 2015 Semi-finals
Published in Journal of Machine Learning Research

IndoWrite

Software that recognizes handwritten Devanagri script and converts it to editable UTF-8 text. 2016

VQA

Research on a program that can answer questions on any image using a neural network to make images accessible to the visually impaired. 2015-present

Created as a part of internship in the Advanced Research Group at Wolfram Research Inc.

Onboard positioning in drone swarms

A hardware attachment for flying machines to perform relative positioning in swarms using radio transcievers. 2016

Sum_Up

Automatic text summarizer in Python based on Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency metric and PageRank. 2015

Built as final project for CBSE Computer Science Grade XII

#tagme

Automatic hashtag generator for Instagram and Facebook pictures using a VGGnet model trained on Instagram data. 2015

Research on a novel bioplastic

Invented a new type of bioplastic based on cross-linked starch polymers extracted from organic waste. 2013

Project selected for national finals of CBSE Science Exhibition

PyDoku

Computer vision based sudoku puzzle solver, made using OpenCV. 2013

Research on automated English accent classification

Built 23-way English accent classifier using a LeNet trained on audio data scraped from internet radios and podcasts with an accuracy of 87%. 2015
Project built as part of Wolfram Tech Innovation Summer Camp 2015

Research on automated speaker identification

Built a speaker identifier based on a classifier trained on wavelet transforms of recorded samples.

Research on phase transitions in Ising models

Investigated phase transitions in two and three dimensional Ising models in presence of magnetic fields using Monte Carlo simulations. 2015-present

App to track spread of epidemics

Created an app to help track spreading of communicable diseases using SMS and mathematically model transmission behavior by disease and vector type. 2014
Google Science Fair 2014

Junk-a-tron

Built a LINAC particle accelerator from discarded material; later converted to a betatron. 2012
Google Science Fair 2012

Research on use of natual limonene as pesticide

Performed research on effects of limonene on crop growth and average pest infestations to show that limonene is a viable natural alternative pesticide. 2013
Google Science Fair 2013

Instacharity Foundation

Worked as Chief Operating Officer at http://instacharity.in. Organised and managed relations with charitable organisations. 2015

The Digilogous

Led the organisation of Digilogous 2014 and 2015 as a co-founder and president. Compiled and hosted the quizzes, and designed the logo and the websites. 2014-2015

Quipu-Lang

Visual programming language based on Quipu/Khipu, the ancient textile based record keeping devices used by the Incas.
